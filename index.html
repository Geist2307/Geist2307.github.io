Who I am

Physicist. Researcher. Data Scientist.

I began my career distinguishing myself in Romania's National Physics Olympiads, trained as a physicist at Southampton, and spent two years as a Junior Research Fellow at the University of Tartu publishing in peer-reviewed optics journals. Recognising the deep overlap between computational imaging and AI, I pivoted fully into data science — completing an MSc with Distinction at St Andrews, where my dissertation produced Bayesian Neural Networks up to 90% smaller than their classical counterparts without sacrificing accuracy. I combine first-principles thinking with hands-on ML engineering to deliver solutions that are both theoretically sound and production-ready.
